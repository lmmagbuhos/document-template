Executive Brief

BLUF Format firecrawl · tavily
Subject
To: Executive Leadership Team
From: Marcus Rivera, VP Engineering
Date: May 19, 2026
BLUF
Request approval to hire 3 additional engineers (cost: $450k/year) to meet Q4 launch deadline; without approval, delivery slips to Q1 2027.
Summary
Platform migration is 6 weeks behind schedule due to unexpected complexity in the payment integration. Current team cannot recover timeline without adding headcount or descoping features.
Background
Q4 launch commitment was based on 8-engineer team. Since then, 2 engineers departed and replacement hiring took 8 weeks. Remaining scope requires 10 engineers to hit original date.
Analysis
Option A Hire 3 engineers (+$450k) — hit Q4 date
Option B Descope payment features — delay 4 weeks, lower quality
Option C Accept delay to Q1 2027
Recommendation
Proceed with Option A. Engineering leadership confirms candidates available; onboarding takes 4 weeks; they can contribute before launch.
Approval Requested
Budget: $450,000 (FY2026 carryover)
Timeline: Decision needed by May 26
Approvers: CEO, CFO

Summary 1-2 sentence overview
Background Context + history
Analysis Options + tradeoffs
